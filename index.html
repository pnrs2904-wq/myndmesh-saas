FAQ

Common Questions

What is MyndMesh and who is it for?
MyndMesh is an AI-first SaaS platform building intelligent products across EdTech, HealthTech, FashionTech, and Enterprise AI. It's for startups, SMEs, and large enterprises that want to integrate AI without building from scratch.
How does the 14-day free trial work?
Sign up with your email — no credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen plan for 14 days. At the end of the trial, you choose a paid plan or your account pauses (your data is retained for 30 days).
What AI models does MyndMesh support?
We integrate natively with OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Meta Llama, and Mistral. You can switch models per use-case from a single dashboard with zero code changes.
Is my data secure and who owns it?
Your data is always yours. We are ISO 27001 certified, SOC2 Type II ready, and GDPR + India PDPA compliant. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never train our models on your data without explicit consent.
Can I get a custom enterprise plan?
Absolutely. We offer tailored pricing for large organizations, white-label deployments, on-premise installations, and custom AI model fine-tuning. Contact our sales team or book a discovery call to discuss your requirements.
